# Pharmaceutical Industry — Business Analysis & Valuation

An end-semester course project for **ECON F355**, prepared for **Prof. Mohit Kumar**.

## What This Project Is About

A deep-dive business analysis and valuation of three major Indian pharmaceutical companies — **Cipla**, **Sun Pharma**, and **Dr. Reddy's Laboratories** — set against the backdrop of India's broader pharma industry.

## What's Covered

- **Industry Overview** — India's $50B pharma market, global export position, growth projections, and key challenges
- **Porter's Five Forces** — Competitive dynamics of the Indian pharma sector
- **Comparative Analysis** — Side-by-side breakdown of business models, segments, and global presence
- **SWOT & BCG Matrix** — Strategic positioning of each company
- **Financial Ratio Analysis** — Profitability, liquidity, leverage, and efficiency metrics (2016–2025)
- **Beta & Risk Analysis** — Systematic risk profiling using regression against NIFTY 500
- **Cost of Equity & Debt** — CAPM-based cost of equity and synthetic credit rating approach for cost of debt
- **WACC Analysis** — Weighted average cost of capital for all three companies
- **DCF Valuation** — Intrinsic value estimates using Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF) over FY25–FY30

## Final Recommendations
